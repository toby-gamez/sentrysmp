About Server

Welcome to SentrySMP

SentrySMP is a survival multiplayer server with an economy and crossplay support. Whether you enjoy building, exploring, or competing, there is something for everyone.

Survival with Economy: Earn and spend money through jobs, trades, and auctions Crossplay Support: Play seamlessly on both Java and Bedrock editions

Jobs and Skills: Choose professions, level up, and unlock rewards

Kits and Crates: Access starter kits and free-to-play crates with amazing rewards

Auction House: Buy, sell, and bid on rare items in a player-driven market

AFK Zone: Stay active and earn rewards while taking a break

Bounties: Set or claim bounties for extra challenges and competition Join SentrySMP today and create your story in a growing and competitive community.
