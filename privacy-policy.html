Privacy Policy

This page is for the Minecraft server website SentrySMP. We respect your privacy and protect your personal data in accordance with applicable laws (including GDPR).

What data we collect:

  • Google Analytics: We track the number of visitors, website behavior, and approximate country location. This data is anonymized and used for analytics only.
  • LocalStorage: We store information such as shopping cart contents, theme, your Minecraft username, previous purchases, and your consent status regarding cookies.
  • Payment Information: When making purchases for spawners, ranks, keys, or VIP status, transaction information is processed by our payment processors (PayPal and Stripe). We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All purchases, including VIP status, spawners, ranks, and keys, can be processed through either PayPal or Stripe according to your preference.
  • Minecraft Username: We collect and store your Minecraft username to deliver purchased items to the correct player account in the game and manage VIP permissions through our server's permission system.

How we use the data:

  • To analyze traffic and improve website functionality.
  • To ensure proper operation of the shopping cart and remember your preferences.
  • To process payments and deliver purchased items (spawners, ranks, keys) or benefits (VIP status) to your Minecraft account. For VIP status, we maintain a record of your purchase date and one-month expiration date.
  • To maintain records of transactions for customer support and administrative purposes.
  • To distinguish between different types of purchases (spawners, ranks, keys, VIP) and apply the appropriate benefits to your account. VIP status requires additional tracking of one-month membership period and automatic management of permissions through our server's RCON interface.

Cookies and Consent:

We use a cookie banner where you can accept or reject the use of Google Analytics. Your choice is saved in your browser’s localStorage under the key cookies-accepted. This policy also applies to any third-party services we use. Currently, we only use Google Analytics, and you can review their Privacy Policy for more information.

Data sharing:

We do not share your data with any third parties, except:

  • Anonymized statistics through Google Analytics.
  • Payment information with our payment processors (PayPal and Stripe) to complete transactions. This includes your Minecraft username, purchase amount, and transaction identifiers.
  • Minecraft usernames and purchased items with our game server to deliver your items and benefits.

Payment processors: Both PayPal and Stripe have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your payment information. We encourage you to review their policies: PayPal Privacy Policy and Stripe Privacy Policy.

Your rights:

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your data. Contact us at our support page.

Please note that transaction records may need to be retained for legal and accounting purposes even if you request deletion of your personal data. For VIP purchases, we must maintain records of your membership status to ensure your benefits remain active for the one-month duration and automatically remove permissions when your VIP status expires.

For data related to payments processed through PayPal or Stripe, you may need to contact these services directly regarding your rights under their respective privacy policies.

Payment Security

All payment transactions are encrypted and processed securely through our payment processors. We do not store credit card information on our servers. Different payment methods may be available for different products:

  • Spawners, Ranks, and Keys: May be purchased through PayPal or Stripe, with transaction data stored only for delivery confirmation and support purposes
  • VIP Status: Can be purchased through either PayPal or Stripe, same as other items. VIP purchases require us to maintain additional data including membership start date, the one-month duration period, and automated permission management through the server's RCON interface which executes /lp user [your_username] clear when your VIP expires

All transaction data is protected during transmission using industry-standard encryption protocols.

Final Provisions

This Privacy Policy is effective from June 22, 2025. It may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
